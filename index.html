Game Concept

Echoes of the Past" is a spine-chilling horror adventure game where players take on the role of Sarah, a determined investigator exploring the eerie Blackwood Manor. Drawn by a mysterious message, Sarah navigates the dark mansion with only her flashlight, uncovering its sinister secrets.

The game features atmospheric horror, intricate puzzles, and supernatural encounters. Sarah discovers six cryptic notes revealing the tragic history of the Blackwood family and the malevolent shadow ghost born from Samuel Blackwood's cursed pact.

Game Genre

"Echoes of the Past" is a horror adventure game that immerses players in a suspenseful, atmospheric experience. Combining elements of psychological horror and mystery, the game challenges players to explore a haunted mansion, solve intricate puzzles, and survive supernatural encounters. With a strong narrative focus and multiple endings influenced by player choices, it offers a compelling mix of exploration, puzzle-solving, and intense horror.

Niche/ Sub-Genre:

First-person horror, Investigation, puzzle-solving
